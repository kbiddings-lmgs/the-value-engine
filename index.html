Discovery Assistant & Report

           

The Value Discovery Buddy is an AI-powered discovery coach and report generator that streamlines your sales discovery, providing deep customer insights and solution positioning before you even step into the meeting.

TYPE: GEM + Content Generator

Tips

To use the Value Discovery Assistant it's easy!

  • Click the Access Assistant button below. That will open the Gem.
  • Say "Hi!" and press Enter. The assistant will start up and you can begin getting to work - that's it.

Save it for later? When in Gemini, you can always PIN the Gem to the left-hand side of your screen to save it for future use.

Guidance: The Assistant is designed to guide you step-by-step to build out your value discovery knowledge about your customer. Once you've finished your conversation and are satisfied with the value discovery process, the assistant will ask you if you'd like to "generate a report." It will provide you with a unique code snippet and a link to the associated Google Sheet generator. The assistant will also give you clear instructions on what to do from there to generate & access your full report.

Make the financial argument undeniable. This suite of tools helps you quantify the value of your solution, allowing you to build and present clear ROI analyses, TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) comparisons, and custom value models that prove the business impact of your product or service.

Proof of Value

Used in the mid-to-late stages of the sales cycle, this Proof of Value (PoV) assistant helps you create an informed hypothesis to bring to a customer, transforming the conversation from a general demo into a customized, one-page plan. It outlines a specific business challenge and defines the exact metrics you will track together, shifting the focus from "what does it do?" to "how will we prove it works for you?" This proactive approach demonstrates your commitment to a measurable outcome and accelerates the deal toward a close.
