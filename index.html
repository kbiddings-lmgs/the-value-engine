Discovery Assistant & Report

           

The Value Discovery Buddy is an AI-powered discovery coach and report generator that streamlines your sales discovery, providing deep customer insights and solution positioning before you even step into the meeting.

TYPE: Gemini Prompt + Content Generator

User Tips

To start the Value Discovery Assistant, follow these simple steps:

  • Click the Gem link to the right to open and start using the assistant in Gemini.
  • Press enter. The assistant will start up and you can begin interacting with it immediately. There's nothing else you need to do to set it up.
  • TIP: you can always PIN the Gem to save it for future use.

HEADS UP: The Assistant is designed to guide you step-by-step to build out your value discovery knowledge about your customer. Once you've finished your conversation and are satisfied with the value discovery process, the assistant will ask you if you'd like to "generate a report." It will provide you with a unique code snippet and a link to the associated generator (this Google Sheet). The assistant will also give you clear instructions on what to do from there to generate & access your full report.
